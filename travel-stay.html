Getting to Venice

By Air

The nearest airport is Venice Marco Polo Airport (VCE), located approximately 13 km from the city center. From the airport, you can reach Venice by:

  • Water taxi (approximately 30 minutes)
  • Alilaguna water bus (approximately 1 hour)
  • Bus to Piazzale Roma, then vaporetto

By Train

Venice Santa Lucia train station is well-connected to major Italian cities and European destinations. From the station, you can easily reach your accommodation by vaporetto or water taxi.

Accommodation

We recommend booking your accommodation well in advance, as Venice can be busy during the summer months. Here are some areas we suggest:

  • San Marco - Central and convenient
  • Cannaregio - Authentic and less touristy
  • Dorsoduro - Artsy and charming
  • Castello - Quiet and residential
